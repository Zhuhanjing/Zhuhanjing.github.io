The guide system of the Institute of Industrial Design

The design of the guidance system is the intermediate subject of traditional architectural design and visual communication. In modern urban life, the information relationship between people and the environment is demanding more and more, as small as an office building, to a large area and even a city requires a scientific and humane navigation design. It is no longer a single design project defined by materials, styling, or processing types, but a system design project integrated into the property operation.

The guidance system of the Institute of Industrial Design is yellow, which is designed to give teachers and students an active and warm atmosphere, and yellow is one of the more striking colors in all colors.The Industrial Design Institute is one of the most important colleges of the Nanjing Academy of Art. He teaches with the concept of green design. In these years of education, there are also obvious manifestations.
